About Us

Welcome to Foodie Hub International Nepal Pvt. Ltd , the official online store of the world-renowned fast-food chain. Foodie Hub International Nepal Pvt. Ltd. is one of the fastest-growing multinational companies in Nepal, a franchisee of Yum! Restaurant International. As a proud franchisee, we are dedicated to bringing you the signature taste and experience right to your doorstep.
Our journey began with a vision to provide our customers with the highest quality food, exceptional service, and an unforgettable dining experience. With the launch of our online store, we are taking that commitment to the next level by offering a seamless and convenient way to order your favorite products from the comfort of your own home.
At Foodie Hub International Nepal Pvt. Ltd, we offer a wide range of products that are made with the freshest ingredients and cooked to perfection. From our signature Original Recipe Fried Chicken to our mouth-watering Zinger Burgers and delicious sides, our menu has something for everyone.
We understand that our customers have busy lives, which is why we have made it our mission to provide a fast, reliable, and hassle-free online ordering experience. With just a few clicks, you can place your order and have it delivered to your doorstep in no time.
We are proud to be part of the family, and we are committed to upholding the brand's values of quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Thank you for choosing Foodie Hub International Nepal Pvt. Ltd , and we look forward to serving you soon!
